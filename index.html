HwpKit

Open sourced HWP editor, made by Team Platypus

Get Involved

Say goodbye to proprietary software.

Dealing with Korean files are hard, especially their HWP format and the obsession with tables. It broke often if you didn't use Hancom Hanword, a proprietary software.

We want to change that, by making a viable open-source alternative to Hancom's Hanword, while retaining key features like table editing, etc.

Get Involved

  • HwpKit saved a ton of time for my HWP parsing project.

Lightweight

By using native frameworks, HwpKit is very light(50MB) compared to commerical softwares, and especially Hanword.

Highly Compatible

Tables no longer break. While that can be the killer feature, HwpKit, based on a port of hwp.js, aims to provide high compatibility between file formats. Strict UTF8 guideline prevents your and other's edits from breaking down.

Fast

Removing useless stuff and powered by Swift 5, HwpKit provides the fastest HWP parsing speeds compared to commercial software, including Microsoft Office and Google Docs.

Better Design

With Fluent Design, HwpKit is a great visual improvement from Hancom's hanword, which (frankly) looks like something from the 2000s.
